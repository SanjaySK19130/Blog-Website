Hey, Wonderful to Meet You

By 2016, our efforts started to pay off as word about our work began to spread. This led to securing our first major client, a regional retail chain. This milestone was a turning point, enabling us to bring our first employee on board. Emma took charge of user experience design, while Liam and I concentrated on coding and managing projects.

Reflecting on our remarkable journey, we decided that hosting a community event to spotlight local tech talent was the perfect way to give back and inspire future innovators. It was a powerful reminder that with passion, teamwork, and a little code, anything can be achieved.
